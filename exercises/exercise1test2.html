Sam the Minuteman

Short Bio

Sam the Minuteman is a junior computer science student at UMass Amherst, known for his school spirit and love of algorithms.

Long Bio

As the proud mascot of UMass Amherst, Sam the Minuteman has taken his passion for community and leadership into the world of computer science. Now in his junior year, Sam spends his days balancing coursework in systems programming, software engineering, and artificial intelligence with his duties cheering on the Minutemen across campus.

When he’s not debugging code or rallying the crowd at a hockey game, Sam enjoys exploring the Pioneer Valley, experimenting with new tech projects, and helping first‑year students feel at home at UMass. His long‑term goal is to combine his love of technology and community engagement by building tools that make campus life more accessible and connected for everyone.
